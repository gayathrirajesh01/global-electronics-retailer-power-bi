Power BI report analyzing revenue decline since 2020 using a Maven Analytics dataset and objectives, exploring trends in products, customers, delivery times, and sales channels to support management insights.

## Business Goals and Objectives
This Power BI report was developed for the Management Team to investigate the decline in company revenue starting in 2020.

## Audience
Management Team

## Specific Questions that needs to be addressed
1. What types of products does the company sell, and where are customers located?
2. Are there any seasonal patterns or trends for order volume or revenue?
3. How long is the average delivery time in days? Has that changed over time?
4. Is there a difference in average order value (AOV) for online vs. in-store sales?

## Frequency of Review
Monthly and Yearly
